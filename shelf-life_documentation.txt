Objective:- to organise the shelf-life of food items

Procedure:-
1. Created a database(Mysql) tables to store the expiry date of food items and to store the items bought by customer respectively
2. Whenever a customer buy something his/her food list will be added in the database of that customer.
3. There is a variable which count the the date passed after an item of a customer is bought.
4. Each day that variable will be update and as soon as it exceeds its expiry date, then that item will be deleted from the customer list with message.
